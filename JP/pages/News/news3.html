SSelected for G-STARTUP (6 batch) sponsored by GLOBIS

Dots for has been selected as one of nine companies as Main Track in the 6th of G-STARTUP, an accelerator program organized by GLOBIS (Chiyoda-ku, Tokyo; CEO: Yoshito Hori).

G-STARTUP Press Release (only in Japanese)

Outline of "G-STARTUP”

Aiming to build a platform to produce 100 unicorn companies, this accelerator program supports startup entrepreneurs who are expected to grow into leading venture companies in Japan in the future. The program includes discussions with a faculty of successful entrepreneurs and lectures that give adopted companies the perspectives and latest management knowledge they need to become unicorns. Mentors, who are active venture capitalists, will support the companies in their efforts to raise funds to become unicorns. Based on an agreement between the selected company and GLOBIS, the G-STARTUP Fund will invest in the company. The total number of investment recipients will be 20.

Since its establishment in 1992, GLOBIS has developed various businesses based on its vision of "creating and transforming society by creating an ecosystem of people, capital, and knowledge related to management. In the area of human resources, we have established the Graduate School of Management, GLOBIS University, GLOBIS Executive School and GLOBIS Management School as educational corporations, GLOBIS Corporate Education as a joint-stock company, and GLOBIS Corporate Education as an in-house training provider, as well as e-learning, online classes, and subscription video learning services. In terms of money, GLOBIS Capital Partners, a venture capital firm that invests in and fosters venture companies; and in terms of knowledge, the publishing business and the GLOBIS information transmission website/application, GLOBIS Digital Platform, which provides e-learning and online classes as well as the subscription-based video learning service GLOBIS Gakubunyu. In terms of "knowledge," GLOBIS is promoting this through its publishing business and the "GLOBIS Chishiroku" information transmission website/application. In addition, GLOBIS Capital Partners operates conferences through G1, a general incorporated association, and KIBOW, a general incorporated foundation, provides earthquake reconstruction assistance and social impact investment to promote creativity and change in society.
